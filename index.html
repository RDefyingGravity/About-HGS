ABOUT THE HARVEY GRAMMAR SCHOOL

What is the Harvey Grammar School?

The Harvey Grammar School is a selective 11–18 boys’ grammar school with a distinguished history dating back to 1674. The school has a potent mix of bright, well-motivated pupils and highly qualified, experienced staff.

Main School Website

What things does the Harvey offer?

The Harvey offers a range of subjects, such as Science, MFL, Music, Geography and PSHE. It also offers extracurricular activities such as Sports and Creative Arts, and hosts clubs such as Chess, Horrible Histories, Orchestra, and Football.
